# Protocol & The Art of Etiquette Services

## Service Overview

We provide tailored advisory and training programs to enhance communication and behavior in both formal and everyday settings for schools, individuals, and royal households, with a focus on personal style, diplomatic presence, and refined interpersonal skills.

## Service Areas

### 1. Royal and Official Protocol Workshops

**Description:** Specialized training in formal protocols for royal households and official settings.

**Target Audience:**
- Royal family members
- Government officials
- Diplomatic personnel
- High-ranking executives

**Key Topics:**
- Royal protocol standards
- Official ceremony procedures
- State visit protocols
- Formal reception guidelines
- Hierarchical seating arrangements
- Gift-giving protocols

### 2. Advanced Hospitality Etiquette Programs

**Description:** Comprehensive training in hospitality standards for high-end service environments.

**Target Audience:**
- Hospitality professionals
- Private household staff
- Hotel management teams
- Luxury service providers

**Key Topics:**
- Guest reception and service
- Table service etiquette
- Fine dining protocols
- Beverage service standards
- Guest accommodation protocols
- Cultural sensitivity in service

### 3. Personalized Communication Strategies

**Description:** Custom-designed communication approaches tailored to individual needs and contexts.

**Target Audience:**
- Business executives
- Public figures
- Social influencers
- Private clients

**Key Topics:**
- Personal branding
- Communication style development
- Cross-cultural communication
- Conflict resolution
- Professional correspondence
- Social media etiquette

### 4. Presence, Articulation & Conversation Training

**Description:** Development of refined interpersonal skills for social and professional settings.

**Target Audience:**
- Emerging leaders
- Public speakers
- Social hosts
- Corporate representatives

**Key Topics:**
- Body language and posture
- Voice modulation and clarity
- Active listening skills
- Conversation techniques
- Public speaking fundamentals
- Social grace and poise

### 5. Foundational Etiquette Programs for Schools

**Description:** Age-appropriate etiquette education for students and educational institutions.

**Target Audience:**
- Primary schools
- Secondary schools
- Private academies
- International schools

**Key Topics:**
- Basic manners and courtesy
- Dining etiquette
- Social interaction skills
- Respect and consideration
- Digital etiquette
- Cultural awareness

## Program Delivery Methods

- **Workshops:** Interactive group sessions

- **Consultation:** Advisory services for specific events or situations
- **Long-term Programs:** Ongoing development courses
- **Assessment:** Pre and post-training evaluations

All programs are designed according to:

Participants will develop:
